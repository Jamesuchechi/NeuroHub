How It Works

From symptom to solution
in minutes, not days.

01

Patient describes symptoms

Text or voice — in any language the patient is comfortable with. No medical jargon needed. The AI understands context, not just keywords.

02

AI assesses & routes

Claude analyses symptom history, scores severity, suggests likely conditions, and decides — self-care or doctor escalation. Instantly.

03

Doctor receives AI brief

The assigned clinician opens a pre-prepared summary. They know the patient's history, chief complaint, and urgency score before typing a single word.

04

CHW monitors & follows up

The patient's health worker sees their updated priority score in real time. If a home visit is needed, they know before the patient calls.
